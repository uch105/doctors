What is it?

This is a all-in-one software for the doctors in Bangladesh. The main goal is to provide a software package so that doctors don't have to use multiple softwares. We have a sophiscated library of Govt. issued drugs, a wide collection of calculators to calculate various calculations, a automated prescription software. Moreover, there is a mini-social platform for doctors where they can ask/discuss about anything. This platform is open for only authorized doctors, interns and students.

What is its parent company?

M/S SiUch Technologies, one of the digital solutions providers operating in Bangladesh, maintains and owns this application. Any inquiry related to this product or legal authority is associated with this company.
